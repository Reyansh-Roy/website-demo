Frequently asked Questions

What is PenseeWeb AI mindmap generator?

Our AI mindmap generator is a free tool that automatically creates mindmaps from YouTube video links or text input. It uses advanced AI algorithms to extract key concepts and organize them into a visually appealing and easy-to-understand mindmap structure.

How do I use the free AI mindmap generator?

Using our AI mindmap generator is simple: 1) Paste a YouTube video link or your text into the input box on our website. 2) Click the 'Generate' button. 3) Enter your email address to claim your mindmap. 4) After a brief ad, your mindmap will be ready to view and download.

Is there a limit to how many mindmaps I can generate?

No, there's no limit! You can generate as many mindmaps as you want for free. After each generation, you'll have the option to create another mindmap immediately.

Do you offer custom mindmap creation services?

Yes, we do! While our AI tool is great for quick mindmaps, we also offer professional, custom-made mindmaps tailored to your specific needs. Click the 'Contact Us' button at the top of our website to submit your requirements, and our team will get back to you.

Why should I use mindmaps?

Mindmaps are powerful tools for learning, brainstorming, and organizing information. They can help you save time, improve memory retention, enhance creativity, and make complex information easier to understand and remember. Our website features many examples and use cases to demonstrate the benefits of mindmapping.
